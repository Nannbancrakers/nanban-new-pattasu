Celebrations with Care

We believe every celebration should be filled with light and laughter. Your safety is our utmost priority. Follow these simple yet essential guide to ensure a hazard-free and spectacular festive season.

The Do's

Safe Practices for Radiant Joy
  • Read Instructions: Always read the instructions printed on the cracker carefully before lighting it.
  • Safe Distance: Keep a safe distance of 5 meters from people and property when using aerial fireworks.
  • Water Bucket: Always keep a bucket of water or sand nearby to handle minor emergencies and dispose of used crackers.
  • Outdoor Only: Light crackers only in open areas, away from electrical wires, vehicles, and flammable objects.
  • Supervision: Ensure children are always under adult supervision when handling or watching fireworks.

The Don'ts

Avoid Hazards for Constant Smiles
  • No Indoor Use: Never light firecrackers inside the house, balcony, or any enclosed space.
  • Don't Relight: If a cracker doesn't ignite or burst, never attempt to relight it. Wait 15 minutes and pour water over it.
  • Keep Away from Pockets: Never carry fireworks in your pocket or touch them immediately after they go off.
  • Don't Experiment: Never try to make your own fireworks or mix powders. Use only factory-sealed products.
  • No Throwing: Never throw firecrackers at people, animals, or moving vehicles. Respect the safety of others.
